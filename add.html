Add to Registry

Install the bookmarklet below, then click it on any product page to add items in one step.

Drag this button to your bookmarks bar:

＋ Add to Registry

How it works

  1. Drag the button above to your browser's bookmarks bar.
  2. Browse to any product page (Amazon, Pottery Barn, wherever).
  3. Click ＋ Add to Registry in your bookmarks bar.
  4. A small window pops up pre-filled with the product name and image. Add price, category, and priority, then hit Save.
  5. The item appears in the registry instantly.
